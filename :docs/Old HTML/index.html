© Blue Skye Stables 2012

Blue Skye Stables is a peaceful, 10 acre, equestrian facility located in Geneva, FL. We offer boarding, training, lessons, horse transport, kid’s program, and summer camp.
Amenities include full size mirrored dressage arena; grass paddocks, jumping field, galloping field, and close proximity to miles of trails.
